\subsection{Clustering Performance and Interpretation}
Clustering reveals that topic-level structure in medical questions is more granular than specialty-level categorization.

\subsection{NER as a Retrieval Quality Gate}
NER rewriting improves Octen-Embedding scores by +0.006 but negatively affects general-purpose models like MiniLM by -0.022.
