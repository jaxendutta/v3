\begin{center}\textbf{Acknowledgements}\end{center}

This research would not have been possible without the guidance, support, and expertise of numerous individuals and organizations. First and foremost, we extend our deepest gratitude to Dr. Arya Rahgozar, whose insights into modern Data Science Applications were instrumental in shaping the methodological approach of this study.

We acknowledge the critical data sources and open-source tools that provided the foundation for our research. Particular thanks are extended to:

\begin{itemize}
    \item The creators of the MedQA-USMLE dataset \cite{jin2021} for providing the professional medical licensing examination questions and textbook corpus
    \item The creators of the MedMCQA dataset \cite{pal2022} for supplying the large-scale multi-subject training data
    \item The SpaCy team and the authors of the \texttt{en\_ner\_bc5cdr\_md} model \cite{neumann2019} for their robust biomedical named entity recognition tools
    \item HuggingFace and the developers of the Qwen3 and Octen models for open-sourcing highly capable LLMs and embeddings
    \item The developers of the BERTopic \cite{grootendorst2022} and Surprise \cite{hug2020} libraries for enabling advanced clustering and recommender system implementations
\end{itemize}
